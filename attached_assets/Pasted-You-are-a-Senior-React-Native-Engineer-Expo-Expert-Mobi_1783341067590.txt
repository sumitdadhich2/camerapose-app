========================================

PROJECT GOAL

Pose Master AI helps users take professional photos by following transparent pose outline templates.

The app is NOT a photo editor.

The app is NOT an AI image generator.

The app works like a smart camera assistant.

Users simply choose a pose template.

The camera opens.

A transparent pose outline appears.

The user fits inside the outline.

Future AI compares the body with the template and captures automatically.

Everything except Login, Subscription and Ads must work offline.
